Application Guidelines

Dear Applicant


Thank you for your interest in Consolidated Bank Ghana (CBG) 2018 Recruitment Exercise. To ensure you are eligible to be shortlisted for applicable vacancies, please log into the recruitment portal using your staff ID or Account number (UniBank only). Upon entry, fill the online questionnaire and attach your most recent curriculum vitae in word or pdf format. All the requested information is mandatory.

Kindly complete and submit your application on or before 29/08/2018. Please be advised no submission will be considered after this date and any submission without the information requested will not be considered.
